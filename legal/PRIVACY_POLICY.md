## 1. INTRODUCTION

MamaCare Health Technologies Limited ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application, website, SMS service, or USSD service (collectively, the "Service").

This policy complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and applicable data protection regulations.

**KEY POINTS:**
- We collect personal and health information to provide the Service
- We use your data to assess symptoms and send emergency alerts
- We do NOT sell your personal data
- You have rights to access, correct, and delete your data
- Emergency contacts are notified automatically when danger signs detected

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

### 3.1 Primary Uses (Essential Services)

We use your information to:

**Provide Core Services:**
- Assess symptoms based on WHO guidelines
- Calculate pregnancy week and due date
- Deliver weekly pregnancy information
- Track your pregnancy progress
- Enable voice symptom checking

**Emergency Response:**
- Detect danger signs automatically
- Send emergency alerts to your contacts
- Share your location with CHW and emergency contacts
- Notify nearest healthcare facilities
- Call ambulance services when needed

**CHW Support:**
- Assign you to a local CHW
- Enable CHW to monitor your pregnancy
- Schedule home visits
- Track CHW performance
- Facilitate communication

**Service Improvement:**
- Analyze which features are most used
- Identify technical issues
- Improve symptom assessment accuracy
- Develop new features

### 3.2 Secondary Uses (With Your Consent)

**Research (Anonymized Data Only):**
- Study maternal health trends
- Improve AI algorithms
- Publish medical research
- Share findings with Ministry of Health
- Contribute to global maternal health knowledge

**You can opt out of research use at any time without affecting core services.**

**Marketing (If You Consent):**
- Send health tips via SMS
- Notify you about new features
- Share success stories
- Offer premium features

**You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.**

### 3.3 Legal Obligations

We may use or disclose your information when:
- Required by Kenyan law
- Ordered by a court
- Requested by law enforcement (with valid legal process)
- Necessary to protect someone's life or safety
- Required by Office of Data Protection Commissioner

## 4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

### 4.1 When We Share (With Your Consent)

**Your Emergency Contacts:**
- Receive alerts when you report danger signs
- Get your location in emergencies
- See summary of symptoms (not full medical history)

**Your Assigned CHW:**

- Can add notes about home visits

**Healthcare Providers:**

### 4.2 When We Share (Without Additional Consent)

**Service Providers:**
We share data with trusted partners who help us operate:

- **Cloud Hosting:** Amazon Web Services (Kenya region)
- **SMS/USSD:** Africa's Talking (message delivery)
- **Payment Processing:** M-PESA/Safaricom (premium subscriptions)
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics (anonymized usage data)
- **Voice Recognition:** Google Cloud Speech API (audio processing)

**All service providers are contractually bound to:**
- Protect your data
- Use it only for specified purposes
- Not share it with others
- Delete it when no longer needed
- Comply with Kenya Data Protection Act

**Legal Requirements:**
We may disclose information when:
- Required by law or court order
- Responding to valid legal process
- Protecting rights, property, or safety
- Investigating fraud or security issues

### 4.3 When We DON'T Share

**We NEVER:**
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data for advertising purposes
- Give your information to data brokers
- Share with third parties for their marketing
- Transfer data outside Kenya without your consent and adequate safeguards

## 5. DATA SECURITY

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards

**Encryption:**
- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords hashed (bcrypt, not stored in plain text)
- Sensitive fields additionally encrypted

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access (you see your data, CHWs see assigned mothers)
- Multi-factor authentication for staff
- Automatic logout after 30 minutes inactivity
- No shared accounts

**Network Security:**
- Firewall protection
- Intrusion detection systems
- Regular security scans
- DDoS protection

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 security monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Audit logs of all data access
- Annual penetration testing

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards

**Staff Training:**
- All staff trained on data protection
- Confidentiality agreements signed
- Background checks for employees with data access
- Regular refresher training

**Policies:**
- Clear data handling procedures
- Incident response plan
- Regular policy reviews
- Data Protection Officer oversight

**Physical Security:**
- Secure data centers (AWS Kenya)
- Access logs for physical servers
- Backup systems in secure locations

### 5.3 Your Role in Security

**You should:**
- Keep your password confidential
- Log out after use (shared devices)
- Report suspicious activity
- Update the app regularly
- Enable device screen lock

## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

**Active Users:**
- Retained while you use the Service
- Retained for duration of pregnancy + 2 years
- Allows postnatal care and follow-up

**After Account Deletion:**
- Personal identifiers removed immediately
- Health data anonymized within 30 days
- Anonymous data may be retained for research

**Legal Requirements:**
- Some data kept longer if required by law
- Financial records: 7 years (tax law)
- Medical records: 5 years (healthcare regulations)

### 6.2 Emergency Contacts

- Retained while you're active
- Deleted when you remove them or close account
- Emergency contacts NOT notified of deletion

### 6.3 CHW Records

- Visit notes retained for 2 years post-pregnancy
- Performance data retained indefinitely (anonymized)
- Can be requested by auditors/regulators

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS (Kenya Data Protection Act)

### 7.1 Right to Access

**You can:**
- View your data in the app (Profile section)
- Request a copy of all your data
- Receive data in portable format (JSON/PDF)

**How:** Settings > Download My Data

### 7.2 Right to Correction

**You can:**
- Update your profile anytime
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request correction of CHW notes (if inaccurate)

**How:** Settings > Edit Profile

### 7.3 Right to Deletion

**You can:**
- Delete your account
- Request all data be erased
- 30-day grace period (in case of mistake)

**How:** Settings > Delete Account

**Exceptions:** We may keep data if:
- Required by law
- Needed for legal claims
- Public health research (anonymized)

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing

**You can:**
- Pause non-essential features
- Opt out of research use
- Stop marketing messages

**How:** Settings > Privacy Preferences

### 7.5 Right to Object

**You can object to:**
- Research use of your data
- Marketing communications
- Automated decision-making (if implemented)

**How:** Email privacy@mamacare.app

### 7.6 Right to Data Portability

**You can:**
- Export your data
- Transfer to another service
- Receive in machine-readable format

**How:** Settings > Export Data

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

**You can:**
- Withdraw consent anytime
- Affects future processing only (not past)
- May limit service functionality

**How:** Settings > Manage Consent
