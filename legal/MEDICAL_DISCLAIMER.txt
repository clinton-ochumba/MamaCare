WHEN TO SEEK MEDICAL CARE

ALWAYS seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

CALL 999 IMMEDIATELY IF YOU HAVE:
• Severe bleeding (soaking a pad in less than 1 hour)
• Severe abdominal pain
• Severe headache with blurred vision or spots
• No fetal movement for 24 hours  
• Seizures or convulsions
• Difficulty breathing
• Chest pain
• Loss of consciousness
• Severe swelling of face, hands, or feet
• Fluid gushing from vagina before 37 weeks
• Fever above 39°C (102°F)

VISIT CLINIC WITHIN 24 HOURS IF YOU HAVE:
• Persistent vomiting (can't keep food/water down)
• Fever between 38-39°C  
• Painful or burning urination
• Unusual vaginal discharge (foul smell, blood-tinged)
• Reduced fetal movement
• Persistent dizziness
• Rapid weight gain (>2 kg in one week)

IN CASE OF EMERGENCY:

1. CALL 999 IMMEDIATELY
2. Do NOT wait for the app  
3. Do NOT rely solely on app advice
4. Seek immediate medical attention
5. The app will alert your emergency contacts but YOU must get medical help
